2 · Core differentiators — the moat big

The defensible layer: deterministic, explainable, multi-variable decision-solving — not "Monte Carlo + sliders."
  • Solve-For mode — named life questions: ① "Can I retire earlier?" (build first) ② "Can we afford this big goal?" ③ (later) "Spend more?" On-demand (one engine job/click); output = a new scenario column; exactly one free lever. next-ish
  • Explainable constraint solver — multi-variable search returning a small Pareto set of least-disruptive paths; auditable replay. Also fixes the solo-vs-combined display confusion.
  • Decision Surface heatmap — retire-age × spend, confidence contours, saved scenarios as clickable points. The best embodiment of "Parallax models interactions." Alternate view in Scenarios.
